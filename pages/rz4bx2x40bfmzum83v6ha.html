My musings are many

This is the post excerpt.

Some of you know me well, some are just getting to know me and some of you haven’t met me yet.  What you all should know is I am not a social media expert – not by a long shot.  I have been known to periodically check my Facebook page and, while I have a LinkedIn and a Twitter account, I have yet to master them, and Instagram and Snapchat are Greek to me.  Many of you have suffered frustrations with me because I don’t keep up with your life experiences and you should know this is equally frustrating for me.  I want to keep up with all of you, I really do, but I know this isn’t possible or even a realistic expectation.

Adding to this frustration, I have been told by experts that, in this environment of social media and internet marketing, a Realtor must not only be knowledgeable, trustworthy and service minded, but he or she needs to create a brand and effectively market that brand online.  So this is my conundrum.  As a Realtor, I need to figure out how to keep up with all of you and keep all of you up with me.

To get started I recently decided to update my Facebook page – to not only create a new brand but to add a fresh face.  This started me thinking and my thoughts led to some revelations and I decided to share these with you. So this is my first official “blog.”  Every month this newsletter will include my new musings.  My hope is that I will be able to translate my thoughts and inner musings into stories that are hopefully as informative as they are entertaining.  Stay tuned…….
