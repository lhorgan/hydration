Notes: 

*** NOTE: Our capacity for the venue is 60. We are currently over capacity. But please sign up if you'd like to participate, we will likely have no-shows remote participants. ***

Philly will be jamming this year under the very shadow of Billy Penn. We will be hosted by the very generous University of the Arts and their Game Art progam.

We will be open for the full 48 hours at Terra Hall, just south of Walnut on S. Broad St. There are ample public transportation options, although parking may be difficult in Center City.

Team-forming ahead of time is not required and, in fact, discouraged. We will be group brainstorming and forming teams at the kickoff.

Workspace, including tables and chairs, power and wifi, will all be provided. There will be couches and a few limited places to crash. Food options are abundant in the area

This is an inclusive, safe event and inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated: http://globalgamejam.org/code-conduct-legal-policies
