Recreation & Parks Advisory Board

Meetings


  • 7 p.m.
  • First Wednesday of each month
  • Falls Church Community Center
    223 Little Falls St.
Note: No meetings in July and August.

Agendas & Minutes


Agendas are available prior to the meetings. Minutes are available following approval. 
Most Recent Agenda
View All Agendas and Minutes

Members


The Board is composed of five members appointed by the City Council for three year terms, with an appointed representative from the Planning Commission and the School Board.

Staff Liaison


  • Daniel J. Schlitt

City Council Liaison


  • Council Member Letty Hardi

School Board Liaison


  • School Board Member Justin Castillo

Overview


Advises the City Council and the Director of the Recreation and Parks Department on matters concerning use of City-owned land, acquisition of land for recreational use, and recreational programs and funding.
