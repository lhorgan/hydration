Adopt cute cats at Diagon Alley in Seattle this weekend

Seattle Humane is holding an adoption event at the Harry Potter-themed display in Ballard on Saturday.
Author: Allison Sundell
Published: 12:46 PM PST January 5, 2018
Updated: 12:49 PM PST January 5, 2018

Cute cats and Harry Potter? Yes, please.

After adopting out six cats at the Diagon Alley Project in Ballard last weekend, Seattle Humane's MaxMobile “Magical Menagerie” will be back on Saturday.

Visitors can meet more adorable cats looking for their forever homes and mingle with Harry Potter characters.

The Magical Menagerie at Diagon Alley in Seattle. Photo: Seattle Humane.

Diagon Alley features replicas of the shops in Harry Potter in the driveway of a Ballard home. It is on display until January 19. After that, the shops will be deconstructed and donated to Camp Korey in Mount Vernon, which supports families dealing with childhood illness and serious medical conditions.

The MaxMobile Magical Menagerie. Photo: Seattle Humane.

Visit the Magical Menagerie at Diagon Alley: 7514 13th Ave NW, Seattle 98117, 3-6 p.m.
